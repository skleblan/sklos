sending to device from PC

0: sync (0x7C)
1: datalength
2: !datalength
3: type = addr-start | data | branchcmd
4: seq (0-255. then resend addr-start, reset to 0)
5: pass=0/fail=!0 (add other codes in future)
6: data[0]
7: data[1]
8: data[2]
9: data[3]
A: data[4]
B: data[5]
C: data[6]
D: data[7]
E: 0x7D
F: checksum

receiving from device to PC
send exact same packet except for pass/fail
(and obviously checksum)
